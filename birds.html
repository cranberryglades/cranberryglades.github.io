Common Birds

Musical Birds:

  • Catharus guttatus (hermit thrush)
  • Poecile atricapilla (black-capped chickadee)
  • Vireo olivaceus (red-eyed vireo)
  • Vireo solitarius (blue-headed vireo, formerly known as the "solitary" vireo)
  • Pipilo erythrophthalmus (eastern towhee, formerly known as the "rufous-sided" towhee)
  • Passerina cyanea (indigo bunting)
  • Dumetella carolinensis (gray catbird)
  • Setophaga caerulescens (black-throated blue warbler)
  • Setophaga virens (black-throated green warbler)
  • Setophaga pensylvanica (chestnut-sided warbler)
  • Cardellina canadensis (Canada warbler)
  • Geothlypis trichas (common yellowthroat)
  • Regulus satrapa (golden-crowned kinglet)
  • Turdus migratorius (American robin)

Noisy Birds:

  • Cyanocitta cristata (blue jay)
  • Corvus brachyrhynchos (American crow)
  • Strix varia (barred owl)
  • Dryocopus pileatus (pileated woodpecker)

Birds You Hear But Rarely See:

  • Troglodytes hiemalis (winter wren)
  • Catharus fuscescens (veery)
  • Sitta canadensis (red-breasted nuthatch)
  • Bonasa umbellus (ruffed grouse) -- Video of a male grouse "drumming"
  • Meleagris gallopavo (wild turkey)
  • Scolopax minor (American woodcock) -- Video of calling bird

Big Birds:

  • Corvus corax (common raven)
  • Ardea herodias (great blue heron)
  • Buteo jamaicensis (red-tailed hawk)
  • Buteo lineatus (red-shouldered hawk)
